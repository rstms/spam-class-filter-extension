This extension will use your email accounts to send and receive brief email messages to the address filterctl@DOMAIN (where DOMAIN is the domain of your email account). It will then delete these messages from your Sent and Inbox folders.

Actions requiring your authorization:

  • Send email to filterctl@DOMAIN
  • Delete the message from your Sent folder (optional)
  • Monitor your Inbox for the reply message (from filterctl@DOMAIN)
  • Read the reply message
  • Delete the reply email from your Inbox. (optional)
